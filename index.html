About Me

Hi, my name is Luciano Orellana, and I enjoy programming and working with large language models. Many of my projects involve the tooling and scaling around these models, in order to optimize things like prompting, storage, and multi-user collaboration. As building converges towards natural language instruction versus traditional programming instruction, I want to contribute towards minimizing friction between user and model, and scaling them for widespread integration.
